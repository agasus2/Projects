How to play: 
To play the game you must press "Play" button in main menu.
Then you must move the wolf to correct  egg spawn. 
You can use keybord(Deffault:, Left top : Q,Left bottom : A, Right top : E, Right bottom : D), 
or buttons in game window. If you broke 3 egg, you lose.
